Glossary

Attitude Control Rockets
Small rockets that are used as active controls to change the attitude (direction) a rocket or spaccraft is facing in outerspace
Escape Velocity
The velocity an object must reach to escape the pull of Earth's graity.
Oxidizer
A chemical containing oxygen compounds that permits rocket fuel to burn both in the atmosphere and in the vacuum of space.
Vernier Rockets
Small rockets that use their thrust to help direct a larger rocket in flight.
